About

This site showcases projects where Brandon worked out something he thought was worth remarking on, hopefully to the benemusment of others. In his free time indoors, Brandon wonders if he's actually fooling anyone by referring to himself in the third person.

Outdoors, he enjoys solo backpacking, camping, hiking, long distance trail running, bicycling, snowshoeing, and shooting cool shots with his camera. Back indoors, he revels in R programming, applied statistics, exploring open source technologies, writing creative non-fiction (think The New Yorker, but not as good) and short stories (think David Foster Wallace or Saunders, but not as good), and driving his Tesla through the dank mountains of the Pacific Northwest (think fast, but faster).

He currently hangs his hat* in Portland, OR.

He computes his maths and science on System76's Thelio Major for scientific computing. RAM: 64GB; Hard drive: 1TB; Processor: 24 threads @ 4.3 GHz; GPU: Radeon RX 590 with 2304 stream processors. It makes him smile.

I'm accepting consulting requests! If you need Data Science advice or work done, feel free to ping me: brandonwdey [at] gmail. I might even do it for free if it's really interesting.

*(Brandon does wear hats, but mainly the unsophisticated baseball variety, not Frank Sinatra-like fedoras crafted of pure panache in Italy, as this might imply.)

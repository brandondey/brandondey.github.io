I Web Therefore I Am

May 10, 2018 | 1 minute read
Share this: Twitter Facebook Pinterest Google+

Yihui Xie (Yee-how Nee) wrote the R package blogdown to create static websites – like this one – using a bifecta of technologies: Hugo and R Markdown.

I'm really impressed. It's super fast, responsive, and has a growing community of developers to bring cool themes into the digital universe. Plus it'll give me the computational flexibility I'm after to showcase analyses that live in R Markdown.

Because this is my first post, I've got to dedicate it to Yihui. And plus, he said something pretty witty in the intro to his (free online) book on blogdown (written in bookdown) that captures one of the reasons I wanted to start a website:

“I web, therefore I am a Spiderman.”

Hyperbole invoked?

Maybe.

But there's an element of truth to the idea that you don't really exist is you don't have a website. This one's mine.

And thanks for building cool technologies, Yihui!
